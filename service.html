My Offerings

Software Architecture

— Ensuring the big picture in mind and provide best solution.
— Consistent guidance on best practices.
— Reduce ambiguity by exploring potential approaches and giving clarity on available solutions.

Systems Design

— Translate business requirements into technical requirements that can be met by a system being designed end-to-end.
— Lead the discussion with the engineering team in exploring potential solutions, while being ultimately accountable for the system's design.

Refactoring and Complexity Reduction

— Ensuring the solution is at a high quality with minimal necessary complexity.
— Code reviews to ensure that we're following good software design practices.
— Observe which components are unnecessarily complex and can be simplified and executing on this effort for the team.

Collaboration and Coordination

— Build consensus and agreement between Program Management, Engineering Management, and other key stakeholders for addressing a business need.
— Working with non-Engineers
— Understanding the Business
— Working with other Engineering teams
